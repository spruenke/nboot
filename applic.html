Nice Applications

Here, I want to provide some nice examples of bootstrap and resampling applications.

Portfolio Analysis - Asset Allocation

Petukhina & Sprünken (2021) propose a semi-parametric resampling technique to compute optimal CVaR portfolios under uncertainty.

Bagging and Random Forests

Breiman (1996) studies the bootstrap aggregation of predictors and estimators. This is the foundation of random forests, which are presented in Breiman (2001).

Pre-Clinical Studies

Konietschke & Pauly (2014), Friedrich et al. (2017), Bathke et al. (2018) and Umlauft et al. (2019) conduct methodological studies of bootstrap- and resampling-based techniques to enhance classical statistics for small-sample problems in the context of translational studies.

Econometrics

Politis & Romano (1994) present a bootstrap for stationary timeseries, which is very useful for econometrics, where timeseries play a very important role.
